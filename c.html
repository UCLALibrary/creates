Explain Concept Map the Introduction

Start by reading the introduction of your chosen article. Then, follow these steps.

Steps

  1. Define key terms.
  2. Identify the research problem or question.
  3. Identify the variables and determine their relationships.

Rationale

Defining key terms will help you identify uncertainties and ensure that you have a full understanding the article. Identifying the research question is important for grasping the article as a whole. Identifying variables and their relationships is key to going beyong definitions and figuring out the way concepts interact.

Guide & Enable Tips & strategies

"I find it helpful to start concept mapping on Post-It notes so I can move them around. Then I transfer everything to a single sheet of paper." - a successful learner

Tips

  • Have lots of scratch paper handy.
  • Read the article once all the way through before starting your map.
  • Prefer to work on a computer? Try Cmap, a free concept mapping software.
